Irina Rurac

Mezzo Soprano

Beginning from childhood, was inspired by the dream to sing, and include music as an important factor in her life. Road map was long, it started from school in a group with musical profile, continued with "Academy of Music, Theater and Fine Arts from Chisinau", Republic of Moldova and finally arrived to the local National Theater of Opera and Ballet "Maria Biesu".

There had her debut with some roles like Cherubino( Nozze di Figaro), Olga (Eugene Onegin) and others. During her activity on stage of her favorite theater, was collaborating with the Philharmonic Orchestra, Orchestra of Academy of Music and soloing in choirs.

One day, following her heart, woke up in Prague, searching for new opportunities to fulfill her desire to sing, joined The National Theater from Prague.

Also, her activity in Opera Ensemble Ticha - Opera, took her to participate in a series of Festivals from different countries like Italy (Trieste), Cyprus and cities of the Czech Republic (Jihlava, Trutnov, Ceske Budejovice).

The important thing that always is leading her behavior in this profession, is that the process of developing and working on a piece of creation is the biggest pleasure, when this attitude is mentioned it means that could be done till boundless.
